WHAT IS CAR FLIPPING?

Car flipping consist of four simple steps:

  1. Finding a car for cheap
  2. Getting parts, tools, and/or chemicals
  3. Repair and/or clean up the car
  4. Resell car for a higher price

People flip cars for profit because it offers a tangible opportunity to earn money by buying vehicles at a low price, often through auctions or private sales, and reselling them at a higher price after repairs or upgrades. Car enthusiasts and those with mechanical skills are particularly drawn to this practice, as it allows them to combine their passion for automobiles with the potential for financial gain. The relatively low entry barriers and flexibility of car flipping—whether as a full-time business or a side hustle—make it an appealing option for many. Success in this field relies on market knowledge and careful management of risks, including legal issues and unexpected repair costs, but the rewards can be substantial for those who navigate these challenges effectively.
